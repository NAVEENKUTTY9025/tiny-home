How long does it take to build a custom tiny home?
Our typical build timeline is 12-16 weeks from design approval to final walkthrough. This includes custom cabinetry, electrical, plumbing, and all necessary inspections. We prioritize quality craftsmanship while ensuring efficient project management — no unnecessary delays, just meticulous tiny home construction.
Do I need a permit for a tiny home?
Permit requirements vary by city and county. Tiny homes on wheels are often classified as RVs, while foundation-built models follow local residential codes. Our team provides zoning assistance and connects you with local permit experts. We’ll help you navigate regulations so your tiny home is 100% compliant.
What's the average cost of a TinyCraft home?
Our standard models range from $49,900 to $89,500 depending on size, finishes, and off-grid capabilities. Fully custom builds start at $65,000. We provide transparent, itemized pricing with no hidden fees — you'll know exactly where every dollar goes, from foundation to fixtures.
Do you offer financing options?
Absolutely! We partner with specialized tiny home lenders offering RV loans, personal loans, and construction-to-permanent financing. Our finance concierge team guides you through pre-approval, flexible payment plans, and low down-payment options — making tiny home ownership accessible.
Where can I park my tiny home?
You have several options: private land (with owner permission), dedicated tiny home communities, RV parks, or as an accessory dwelling unit (ADU) on a family property. We provide an up-to-date directory of tiny-friendly locations nationwide and assist with zoning research for your specific area.
Can a tiny home accommodate a family?
Yes! Our larger models (400–600 sq ft) feature multiple lofts, clever bunk rooms, and smart storage solutions. We specialize in family-friendly floor plans that comfortably sleep 2–4 people while maintaining privacy and functional common areas. Many families thrive in thoughtfully designed tiny spaces.
Are TinyCraft homes eco-friendly?
Sustainability is at our core. We use FSC-certified timber, low-VOC paints, energy-efficient appliances, spray foam insulation, and solar-ready electrical systems. Many of our models achieve net-zero energy usage with optional solar packages — reducing your carbon footprint significantly.
Can I go off-grid with my tiny home?
Definitely. Our off-grid package includes high-efficiency solar panels, lithium battery storage, composting toilets, rainwater catchment, and propane backup. Perfect for remote locations or self-sufficient living. You'll enjoy energy independence without sacrificing modern comfort.
Do you deliver tiny homes nationwide?
Yes, we deliver to all 50 states! Our logistics team manages transportation permits, route planning, and on-site placement using professional flatbed carriers. Delivery costs vary by distance, and we provide a firm quote before production begins.
